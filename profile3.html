About

Dr. Neeraj Agarwal holds a Bachelor of Dentistry degree from Sharad Pawar Dental College, Wardha. He then pursued his Master's Degree in Orthodontics from D.Y. Patil University, Pune. During his residency years, he completed a range of Orthodontic Certifications in Clinical Photography, Micro-Implants, and Lingual Orthodontics. After obtaining his MDS, he worked as a Consultant Orthodontist at Wonder Smile Clinic, Pune. His passion for Aesthetics led him to pursue his Fellowship in Cosmetic and Aesthetic Dentistry from the prestigious ENCODE Institute in Mumbai. To stay updated, he regularly attends various conferences and conventions.
